What is Covid Controller?

Covid Controller is a web app based on a simple model of disease transmission. It allows anyone to control the inputs that determine how a disease will spread.

The goal is to improve understanding and intuition of this very complicated situation.

What Covid Controller is NOT

Covid Controller is not intended to predict the outcome of the covid19 pandemic.

The dynamics of disease transmission are fundamentally challenging. Small changes in behavior can result in massive swings in outcomes.

This challenge is a double edged sword:
  • Even professionals with the best data and models have little chance of accurately predicting the outcome.
  • We can be informed by these models and our individual actions can have large effects.
Why did I create this?

Since the beginning of the Covid19 pandemic, we have all been overloaded with information. Without any context, it is difficult to digest and we all have a lot of questions.

Running these experiments allowed me to answer basic questions and improved my understanding of disease spread. I thought it might be useful for others to use as well, so I turned that model into CovidController.com.
